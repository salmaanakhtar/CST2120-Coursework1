Game Rules

Welcome to the game! Here’s a quick overview of how to play:

Objective

Click on moles to score points. Avoid clicking on the cats!

How to Play

  • Click "Play" to start the game.
  • Moles will appear randomly in the holes — click them to score.
  • Avoid clicking on cats, or the game will end!
  • If you miss or click an empty hole, you lose a life.

Lives & Waves

You start with 3 lives. Each wave gets faster, and moles appear more often. Try to survive as long as possible!

Game Over

The game ends if you lose all your lives or click on a cat. Your score will be saved and added to the leaderboard.

Make sure you're logged in to play and save your score!
